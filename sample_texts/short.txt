The field of information security has grown significantly in recent decades. Modern digital
communication systems rely on cryptographic protocols to protect sensitive data from unauthorized
access. These systems use mathematical algorithms to transform plaintext into ciphertext, ensuring
that only authorized parties can read the original message.

Beyond encryption, steganography offers an alternative approach to secure communication by
concealing the existence of a message within an ordinary carrier medium. This technique embeds
secret data inside images, audio files, or text documents in ways that are imperceptible to
human observers and difficult for automated detection systems to identify reliably.
